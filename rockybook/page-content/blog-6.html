How to Win at Teen Patti

Teen Patti is a popular card game that originated in South Asia, similar to poker but simpler. The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards (without jokers). A player receives three cards, and the goal is to have the best hand, following a set ranking of cards. The player must place bets, either playing “seen” (looking at their cards) or “blind” (without looking). The player or the dealer with the highest-ranking hand at the end wins the pot.
