Recognizing the Risks

Gambling, including Live Betting and Betting Markets, is a form of entertainment, but it can become problematic for some individuals. It's essential to recognize the signs of problem in gambling, which may include:

Chasing losses: Persistently betting to recover lost money.

Neglecting responsibilities: Spending excessive time on Betting Odds or other gambling activities, leading to neglect of work, family, or social obligations.

Borrowing money to gamble: Taking loans or using funds meant for other purposes to continue gambling.

Emotional distress: Feeling stressed, anxious, or depressed due to gambling.

If you notice any of these signs in yourself or someone you know, it may be time to seek help.

Tools for Responsible Gaming

If you need a break from gambling, you can request a temporary or permanent self-exclusion from our customer service. During the self-exclusion period, you will not be able to access your account or participate in any betting activities.

Protecting Minors

At RockyBook, we take the protection of minors seriously. We do not allow individuals under the age of 18 to participate in any of our betting activities, including Betting Exchanges. We use various age verification methods to ensure compliance. Additionally, we encourage parents and guardians to use parental control tools to restrict access to gambling sites for minors.

Responsible Gaming Tips

  1. Set a budget: Decide how much money you can afford to lose before you start betting, and stick to that amount.
  2. Don’t chase losses: Accept that losing is part of gambling, and never bet more to recover lost money.
  3. Balance your life: Ensure that gambling does not interfere with your personal life, work, or relationships.
  4. Take regular breaks: Ensure that gambling does not interfere with your personal life, work, or relationships.
  5. Avoid gambling when stressed or upset: Negative emotions can impair your judgment, leading to reckless betting.
